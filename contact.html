ABOUT ME

Eventually I would like to get more content here, some about my history and my personal hobbies. For now it will contain an informal introduction. Before this degree, I had got to University of Alaska for physics and had hoped to find an opportunity in physics education. As things would have it, plans ended up changing and I navigated the job market as a physicist with a bachelor's degree. This seemed to highlight the fact that no one knows exactly what a physicist does, even physicists themselves. This gave time to think about what skills would help utilitize my existing skills and have a clear, desirable skill. This was when I discovered the Post-bachelor program at OSU. Really enjoying it so far, although doing the fast-track is a bit like wrestling a tornado.

I love to play music and had an amazing opportunity to be part of the fairbanks music scene. It was then I came to discover my love for the bass guitar and haven't put it down since. In my free time, I continuously find new projects, from tinkering with electronics (hoping to build a 3-d printer and post on here), making soundtracks for friends movies and podcasts, and continously trying to master programing in a way that I can share it with others in a practical sense.
